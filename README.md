<h1 class="title">The System That Keeps Us Poor</h1>

<div class="line"></div>

<div class="poem">
They call it poverty,
as if it were a choice,
as if empty plates
and sleepless nights
were something we created ourselves.

We wake before the sun,
work until our hands forget what rest feels like,
yet somehow,
the finish line keeps moving
farther away.

They tell us to dream bigger,
while building walls around every opportunity.

They call us lazy,
without seeing the weight
we have carried since childhood.

Some inherit wealth.
Others inherit hunger.

One child is born with endless chances,
another is born learning
that survival comes before dreams.

This is more than poverty.
It is a system
that rewards privilege
and punishes those
who started with nothing.

But every empty stomach
still carries a heartbeat.
Every tired soul
still carries a dream.

And one day,
the voices they ignored
will become too loud to silence,

because no one was ever meant
to spend their whole life
simply trying to survive.
</div>
